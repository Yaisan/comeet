This is a presentation page for our work of synthesis of 2 SMX a work whose purpose is to complete the integration of the contents taught throughout the cycle, globalizing and interrelating and, where appropriate, completing, those contents likely to show the degree of achievement of the general objectives, at the end of the training cycle

Our goal is to provide schools with a standard for conducting virtual classes. Therefore, by conducting research we have come to the conclusion that the best solution is to use Jitsi meet a free open source system for video calls.
That's why we created comeet, a convenient tool to install and configure jitsi meet in an easy and free way.
